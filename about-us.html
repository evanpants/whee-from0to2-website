About Us

Meet the team behind whee!

Our Mission

Whee provides products and services to make cities safer and families' lives more convenient.

Ed Casabian

Ed is an operations leader who was an early Uber employee who launched NYC. He then started Climate Draft to create a pathway for skilled talent to apply their skills to meaningful climate companies.

Evan Abel

Evan is a mechanical engineer and product designer, who has launched dozens of products at OXO, Doona, Wim... He has an MBA and leads at the intersection of Design, Product, and Operations. He launched Walmart InHome, led New Verticals at The Rounds, and steered CowBell Labs.

How We Started

Ed and Evan were introduced by a friend because of their interest in sustainability. Over months of geeking out about bicycles, and asking questions about why NY wasn't as filled with bikes as they thought it should be, they decided to join forces and help families discover the incredible convenience and daily life change available with a family bike.
